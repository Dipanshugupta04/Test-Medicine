Preparation Guidelines

How to prepare for your imaging procedure

General Preparation
  • Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing
  • Avoid wearing jewelry or metal objects
  • Bring your ID and insurance information
  • Arrive 15 minutes before your appointment
  • Inform us if you might be pregnant
Procedure-Specific Instructions

No food or drink for 4 hours before the procedure. Inform us about any metal implants or devices.

Fast for 3-4 hours before the scan. You may be asked to drink a contrast solution.

Drink 4-6 glasses of water 1 hour before your appointment and avoid emptying your bladder.
