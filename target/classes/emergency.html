Emergency Instructions
Signs of Medical Emergency:
  • Chest pain or pressure lasting more than a few minutes
  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • Sudden severe pain (especially head, abdomen, or back)
  • Sudden dizziness, weakness, or change in vision
  • Severe bleeding that won't stop
  • Sudden confusion or difficulty speaking
  • Unconsciousness or unresponsiveness
  • Seizures lasting more than 5 minutes
First Aid Basics:
  • CPR: 30 chest compressions followed by 2 rescue breaths
  • Choking: Perform abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver)
  • Bleeding: Apply direct pressure with clean cloth
  • Burns: Cool with running water, don't use ice
  • Fractures: Immobilize the area, don't try to realign
  • Poisoning: Call poison control immediately
